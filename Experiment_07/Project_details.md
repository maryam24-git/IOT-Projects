## RC Charging Circuit
Objective: To build an RC circuit, measure the voltage across the capacitor over time, and plot the voltage vs. time graph.

### Circuit Components
- **Power Supply:** 5V DC
- **Resistor:** 1 kOhm (1k $\Omega$)
- **Capacitor:** 1000 $\mu$F (Electrolytic)
- **Switch:** Slide Switch
- **Measurement Tools:** Multimeter (to record data) and Oscilloscope (to visualize the charging curve)

### Circuit Setup
1. The **Power Supply** is connected to the **Slide Switch**.
2. The switch feeds into the **Resistor**, which is in series with the **Capacitor**.
3. The **Capacitor** is connected to the ground (GND).
4. A **Multimeter** is connected in parallel with the capacitor to record instantaneous voltage.
5. An **Oscilloscope** is connected in parallel to plot the charging curve in real-time.
